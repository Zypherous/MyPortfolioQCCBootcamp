Understanding

 The goal of this website is to help others achieve a better understanding of one another to create a better world. Understanding is an application of Empathy along with knowledge.

 Often, people have misunderstandings when communicating with one another. This happens due to the different environments, social-economic upbringings, education and many other uncontrollable factors. By arming oneself with knowledge, one is able to educate and themself, and others to facilitate and have more amicable discourse between eachother.

About Understanding from the Author:

 My name is Jonathan. Im a firm believer in understanding one another through knowledge and education. By putting yourself in another person's shoes, you can better understand their view point and foster a better relationship. If everyone is able to have positive interactions and communicater with eachother, it will lead to a better world overall. This opens an avenue for improving the lives of those around your and by extension yourself.
