My name is Jonathan. Im an aspiring software engineer and web developer. My journey has taken me to many places, jobs, classes, and ordeals. With persistence I have managed to overcome and pursue my childhood dream to work with computers. Never let your dreams die because with enough persistence, hard work, and a "can-do" attitude, you can accomplish anything!

Im a senior at Lehman college graduating this summer. Working various jobs in the non-profit sector, I decided a career change was needed. Currently I manage a property, study in school, study on my own, work out, play games, and enjoy a book or two every now and then.

My goal is to gain experience on the field, and eventually enter the XR field. For those who are unfamiliar with XR, it stands for "Extended Reality" and encompasses VR(virtual reality), AR(augmented reality), and MR(mixed reality). I plan on building my website slowly, getting a blog up and running, and continue to read and learn more about the XR field. An html5 XR app sounds like it will be a fun project to work on when I graduate ;)
